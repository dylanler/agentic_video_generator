In the gleaming halls of VirtualTech, innovation meets memory. A breakthrough allows us to step through time, recreating moments lost to history with perfect clarity. But beneath the polished surface, darkness lurks.

When Ethan uncovers disturbing patterns in the Montague estate's past, what begins as historical research spirals into obsession. Through virtual reality, he witnesses a tragic death from 1938 - a young woman's fatal fall that was never explained.

Now, confronting Elizabeth Montague in the present, Ethan realizes some family secrets refuse to stay buried. As history threatens to repeat itself, the truth about the Montagues emerges, proving that technology can reveal the past - but can't change it.
