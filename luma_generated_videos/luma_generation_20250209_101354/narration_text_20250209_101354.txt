In a world where memories can be rebuilt and relived, one man's curiosity becomes an obsession. Ethan Montague discovers Family Reunion, a groundbreaking technology that brings the past to life with stunning precision. Through advanced AI and virtual reality, he steps into his family's storied 1930s estate, meeting ancestors he never knew.

But beneath the champagne and laughter of a seemingly perfect gathering, dark secrets begin to surface. As Ethan digs deeper into his family's history, he uncovers a web of conspiracy and murder that's been buried for generations.

Now, trapped between past and present, he must confront a shocking truth that threatens to destroy not just his family's legacy, but his own reality. Some memories were meant to stay buried.
