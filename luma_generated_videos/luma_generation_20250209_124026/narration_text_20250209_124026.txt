In a world where memories become reality, technology opens doors to our deepest desires. Through advanced virtual systems, we can step into moments lost to time. But as Ethan discovers while researching the Montague estate, some doors were meant to stay closed. A tragic death in 1930 haunts these virtual halls, where champagne flowed and secrets festered beneath polite society's veneer. Now, as past and present collide, Elizabeth Montague guards her family's darkest truth. In this dangerous game of virtual reality and historical truth, history threatens to repeat itself on those same marble steps where tragedy first struck decades ago.
