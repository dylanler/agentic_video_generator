In a world where the past can be perfectly recreated, Family Reunion promises the impossible - a chance to step back in time and embrace lost loved ones once more. For writer Ethan Montague, this technological marvel becomes an obsession as he delves into his family's storied history.

But beneath the champagne-soaked glamour of a 1930s lakeside estate party, dark secrets begin to surface. What starts as an innocent exploration of his heritage through revolutionary AI technology transforms into something far more sinister.

As Ethan uncovers layers of carefully buried truths, he realizes some memories were meant to stay forgotten. Now, trapped between past and present, he must confront a shocking revelation that threatens not only his family's legacy but his very survival.

In the Montague mansion, history isn't just repeating itself - it's coming alive with deadly consequences.
