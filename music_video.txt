An Akira-inspired music video set in an 80s cyberpunk vaporwave universe. 
The story follows a neon-haired girl who teams up with a mysterious rider on a sleek hoverbike. 
Together, they embark on a thrilling escape from a dystopian city, weaving through towering skyscrapers and dodging holographic billboards. 
Their journey takes them beyond the neon-lit streets, soaring into the cosmos, where they navigate through the stars and space debris. 
The climax of their adventure sees them arriving at the majestic gas giant, Jupiter, with its swirling storms and hypnotic colors. 
The artistic style is a mesmerizing blend of vaporwave and anime style, cyan blue purple pink
